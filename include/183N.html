183 North Mobility Project

The 183 North Mobility Project includes construction of two express lanes in each direction along a 9-mile stretch of US 183 between SH 45/RM 620 to Loop 1, express lane direct connectors to and from southbound Loop 1, and the addition of a fourth general purpose lane, where required, to bring the total number to four in each direction. The project also includes operational improvements to Loop 1, new shared use path connections, new sidewalks, and cross-street connections for bicycles/pedestrians.

  • Phase: Under Development
  • Project Cost: $500 Million
  • Project Length: Approx. 9 miles along US 183
  • Opening Date: Early 2025
  • Toll Rate: Variable
  • Shared Use Path: 1 mile (TBD)
