290/130 Flyovers project

The Mobility Authority constructed three flyovers between the SH 130 and 290 Toll facilities. These flyovers give drivers a free-flowing direct connection and free up capacity at the frontage road intersection underneath the toll facilities for drivers who prefer the non-tolled option.

  • Phase: Open to traffic
  • Project Cost: $127 Million
  • Project Length: 290 Toll/SH 130 Intersection
  • Opening Date: 2021

DID YOU KNOW?
  • The flyovers will shift approximately 67% of vehicles off the southbound SH 130 frontage road and onto the new flyover ramp to westbound 290 Toll.
  • The north-to-west and south-to-west flyover bridges are named in honor of James H. Mills, former Mobility Authority board member who was an advocate for the flyovers.
