290/130 Flyovers project

The Mobility Authority will construct three flyovers between the SH 130 and 290 Toll facilities. These flyovers will give drivers a free-flowing direct connection and free up capacity on the frontage road intersection underneath the toll facilities for drivers who prefer the non-tolled option.

  • Phase: Under Construction
  • Project Cost: $127 Million
  • Project Length: 290 Toll/SH 130 Intersection
  • Opening Date: 2021 (Anticipated)
  • Toll Rate: TBD
  • Average Daily Traffic: TBD
  • More Information can be found at 290Flyovers.com (Opens in a new tab/window)
DID YOU KNOW?
  • The project will attract up to 5,000 vehicles per day from parallel facilities providing ancillary congestion relief to other non-toll corridors.
  • The project will shift approximately 67 percent of vehicles off the southbound SH 130 frontage road and onto the new flyover ramp to westbound 290 Toll.
  • Former Mobility Authority board member James H. Mills was an advocate for the 290/130 Flyovers; the north-to-west and south-to-west flyover bridges will be named in his honor.
