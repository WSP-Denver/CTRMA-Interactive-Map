183A Toll Phase III

The proposed 183A Phase III tollway project will have two tolled lanes in each direction to start, and will ultimately be widened to three lanes, to help relieve congestion and enhance mobility for residents of Cedar Park, Leander, and Liberty Hill. The 6.6-mile stretch will be an extension of the existing 183A Toll north from Hero Way to north of SH 29.

  • Phase: Under Development
  • Project Cost: $250-260 Million
  • Project Length: 6.6 miles
  • Construction Start: Anticipated late 2020
  • Opening Date: Anticipated in 2024
  • Shared Use Path: 4.6 Miles
  • Toll Rate: TBD
  • Average Daily Traffic: TBD
  • More Information can be found at www.183A.com
DID YOU KNOW?
  • With unprecedented growth in Williamson County, most notably in Cedar Park, Leander, and Liberty Hill, traffic volumes along US 183 are anticipated to increase by 200 percent over the next 25 years.
  • The 183A Phase III project is currently undergoing environmental analysis, and the public has the opportunity to review and comment on the proposed plans on the project website.
  • The extension of 183A Toll includes a shared use path north from Hero Way to the proposed Seward Junction Loop project.
