183A Toll Phase III

A planned 6.6-mile extension of the 183A Toll Road from Hero Way to north of SH 29 will improve mobility for residents of Cedar Park, Leander, and Liberty Hill. Final design is underway for the project, which will initially include constructing two tolled lanes in each direction, with plans to widen to three lanes in the future.

  • Phase: Under Development
  • Project Cost: $260 Million
  • Project Length: 6.6 miles
  • Construction Start: Early 2021
  • Opening Date: Anticipated in 2024
  • Shared Use Path: 4.6 Miles
  • Toll Rate: TBD
  • Vehicles Per Day: TBD
  • More Information can be found at www.183A.com (Opens in a new tab/window)
DID YOU KNOW?
  • With unprecedented growth in Williamson County, most notably in Cedar Park, Leander, and Liberty Hill, traffic volumes along US 183 are anticipated to increase by nearly 200 percent over the next 25 years.
  • The 183A Phase III project received its environmental finding in August 2019, and is currently undergoing final design.
