45SW Project

45SW is an innovative four-lane toll road built on state property between MoPac and FM 1626 that is bringing relief to the rapidly growing area of northern Hays and southern Travis counties. This project balances environmental sensitivity with new infrastructure to manage congestion in the region.

  • Phase: Open to Traffic
  • Project Cost: $108 Million
  • Project Length: 3.6 miles
  • Opening Date: June 2019
  • Toll Rate: $1, Higher rates apply for Pay By Mail
  • Shared Use Path: 4.5 miles
  • Average Daily Traffic: 9000
  • More Information can be found at www.sh45sw.com
DID YOU KNOW?
  • Our Grand Opening Jubilee event was a great success with many members of the public joining us for the celebration.
  • 45SW is now open to the public with more than 9,000 cars using the road daily.
  • 45SW features a beautiful shared use path with informational signage that informs pedestrians and bikers about the history and wildlife of the region.
  • The 45SW project team was honored to receive the 2018 Brookfield Residential Environmental Champion Award by the Austin Chamber of Commerce for the project's investment in sustainability, innovation, and leadership in the transportation industry.
